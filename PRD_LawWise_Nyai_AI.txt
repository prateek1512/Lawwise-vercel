2. PROBLEM STATEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  - Time Waste:              Lawyers spend 5-8 hours daily on repetitive
                             research, drafting, and document review.

  - Low Leverage:            70% of a lawyer's time is consumed by manual,
                             low-leverage workflows.

  - Client Discovery:        Difficulty finding consistent, high-quality clients.

  - Limited Visibility:      Limited visibility in a crowded legal market.

  - No Performance Proof:    No structured way to showcase verified case
                             performance.

  - Word-of-Mouth Dependency: Reliance on word-of-mouth instead of data-backed
                             credibility.

  - Reputation Barrier:      Young lawyers struggle to build reputation despite
                             strong capabilities.

Core insight: Many capable lawyers lose opportunities because potential clients
cannot easily discover or evaluate them.

4. PRODUCT FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4.1 AI Legal Assistant

  AI Legal Research:
    - Get case laws and relevant precedents in minutes
    - Context-aware legal search

  Smart Drafting:
    - Generate petitions, notices, agreements instantly
    - Customizable jurisdiction-based formats

  Case Analysis:
    - Summarize judgments & extract key arguments
    - Identify winning patterns from past rulings

  Enterprise-Grade Security:
    - End-to-end encrypted data at rest & in transit
    - Secure, SOC2-ready cloud architecture

4.2 Lawyer Intelligence Network (LIN)

  India's first AI-powered Lawyer Intelligence Network
  "Where Legal Performance Meets Data Transparency."

  Upload Case Records:
    - Add case details manually or upload judgments
    - Categorize as won, lost, or ongoing

  Performance Analytics:
    - AI-generated win-rate analysis
    - Case-type breakdown & historical insights

  Expertise Tagging:
    - Criminal, Corporate, Civil, Constitutional
    - Arbitration, IP, and custom domains

  Verified & Secure:
    - Encrypted document handling
    - Lawyers control what is publicly visible

  LIN Onboarding Flow (3 Steps):
    Step 1 — Create Your Profile
    Step 2 — Upload Case Records & Expertise
    Step 3 — Let AI Generate Structured Legal Insights

  Value Proposition:
    - Lawyers gain visibility
    - Clients gain transparency
    - The legal ecosystem becomes smarter

  Compliance: All uploaded data remains encrypted and fully controlled by the
  lawyer. LawWise does not publicly disclose confidential client information
  without consent.
